Bobbatlu

Bobbatlu, also known as Puran Poli, is a traditional Indian sweet flatbread, popular in Andhra Pradesh, Maharashtra, Karnataka, and Telangana. It consists of a sweet filling, often made with chana dal (split chickpeas) and jaggery, encased in a dough made from flour. Bobbatlu is a festive dish, often enjoyed during celebrations and special occasions.
